about the Leaf

The Oberlin Leaf was created to fill a perceived vacuum at Oberlin for publications that pursue the ugly truth.

We see that existing media corporations at Oberlin serve dark and ancient established interests, and we strive to disrupt this environment with furiously independent journalism.

We’re dedicated to telling the other side of the story - the stories that have never been told, are not being told, and probably will never be told if we don’t tell them ourselves.

We also publish fiction, poetry and visual art - see Stories and Tidbits.
